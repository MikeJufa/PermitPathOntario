How We Work

A Process Designed
for Clarity

01

Discovery & Brief

Deep consultation to understand programme, site, budget, and timeline. Goals defined precisely before drawing begins.

02

Concept Design

Exploration of spatial strategies, massing, and aesthetic direction. Multiple options refined through open dialogue.

03

Design Development

Approved concept evolved into detailed drawings, specifications, and material schedules for structural coordination.

04

Documentation

Complete construction drawing sets and submission packages prepared to satisfy authority requirements.

05

Construction Administration

Site observation, contractor coordination, and shop drawing reviews ensuring the built result honours the design.

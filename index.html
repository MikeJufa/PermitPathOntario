How we work

A clear path from
vision to built form

Every project follows the same five stages — adapted to the specific scale, complexity, and ambition of your brief.

Step 01

Discovery & Brief

Deep consultation on programme, site, budget, and timeline. Goals defined precisely before drawing begins.

Step 02

Concept Design

Spatial strategies and aesthetic direction explored. Multiple options presented and refined through dialogue.

Step 03

Design Development

Approved concept evolved into detailed drawings, specifications, and material schedules.

Step 04

Documentation

Complete construction drawing sets prepared to satisfy municipal authority requirements.

Step 05

Construction Admin

Site observation, contractor coordination, and shop drawing reviews to protect the design.

The studio

Integrity in every line

FORMA Studio is an independent architecture and design consultancy founded on the belief that thoughtfully designed spaces genuinely improve lives. We bring together rigorous technical expertise and a deeply human sensibility to every project we undertake.

Our principals carry decades of combined experience across residential, cultural, commercial, and urban-scale projects — always maintaining the same standard of care regardless of budget or programme size.

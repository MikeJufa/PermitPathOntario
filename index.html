How we work

Permit in hand, stress-free

We manage every step so you don't have to chase the City. Most residential permits in Mississauga are issued within 4 – 8 weeks of a complete submission.

Step 01

Free Feasibility Check

We review your address, zoning by-law, and project idea at no charge — and tell you exactly what's possible before you spend a dollar.

Step 02

Site Measurement

We visit your property and take precise laser measurements. Existing conditions are documented in AutoCAD as the foundation for all drawings.

Step 03

BCIN Drawing Set

We produce a complete OBC-compliant drawing package and sign it with our BCIN number — no architect or engineer required for Part 9 homes.

Step 04

City Submission

We submit the application, pay on your behalf, and track the file. We handle all examiner comments and resubmissions until approval.

Step 05

Permit Issued

You receive the permit and are ready to build. We remain available to answer contractor questions throughout construction.

About us

Your local BCIN expert

Permit Path Ontario is a BCIN-registered design practice based in Mississauga, serving homeowners and contractors across the Peel Region and Greater Toronto Area. We specialise exclusively in residential building permit applications under Part 9 of the Ontario Building Code.

Because we hold our own BCIN number, we can independently prepare, sign, and submit permit drawings — cutting out the architect or engineer middleman and passing those savings directly to you. Our clients are homeowners adding basement suites, contractors needing fast drawing turnarounds, and realtors assessing expansion potential before an offer.
